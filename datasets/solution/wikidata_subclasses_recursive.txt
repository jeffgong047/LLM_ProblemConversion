natural science: branch of science about the natural world
science: systematic enterprise that builds and organizes knowledge, and the set of knowledge produced by this enterprise
knowledge system: systems of knowledge produced over time through interactions with other human beings
conceptual system: system composed of non-physical objects, i.e. ideas or concepts
system: set of interacting or interdependent components
collective entity: entity that is a composite of other entities
non-physical entity: object that exists outside physical reality
object: anything that may be acted upon by a subject
artificial entity: anything created by humans (either material or mental)
output: what comes out of something (input)
