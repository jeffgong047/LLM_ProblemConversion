profiles: In an attempt to escape from the negative conclusion of Arrow's theorem, social choice theorists have investigated various possibilities ("ways out"). This section includes approaches that deal with aggregation rules (functions that map each preference profile into a social preference), and other functions, such as functions that map each preference profile into an alternative. Since these two approaches often overlap, we discuss them at the same time.
