For example for n=2=2 that would be f(X1,X2)=X1⋅f(1,X2)+X1′⋅f(0,X2)=X1X2⋅f(1,1)+X1X2′⋅f(1,0)+X1′X2⋅f(0,1)+X1′X2′⋅f(0,0)f(X_1,X_2) =X_1(1,X_2)+X_1'(0,X_2) =X_1X_2(1,1)+X_1X_2'(1,0)+X_1'X_2(0,1)+X_1'X_2'(0,0) Likewise, application of the dual form leads to the Product of Sums (PoS) canonical form (using the distributivity law of ++ over ⋅·): f(X1,X2)=(X1+f(0,X2))⋅(X1′+f(1,X2))=(X1+X2+f(0,0))⋅(X1+X2′+f(0,1))⋅(X1′+X2+f(1,0))⋅(X1′+X2′+f(1,1))f(X_1,X_2) =(X_1+f(0,X_2))·(X_1'+f(1,X_2)) =(X_1+X_2+f(0,0))·(X_1+X_2'+f(0,1))·(X_1'+X_2+f(1,0))·(X_1'+X_2'+f(1,1)) Properties of
